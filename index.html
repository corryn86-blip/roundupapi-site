01 The problem

The demand is proven. The tools quietly take a cut of it.

Shoppers want to give at checkout, and merchants want to offer it. But the popular Shopify plugins charge 10–13% taken directly from the donation — so the charity receives less than the customer intended, and the merchant is locked into a single charity network with no infrastructure underneath. It works, but it skims the gift, and that's a trust problem hiding in plain sight.

02 Our approach

Round-ups, rebuilt as financial infrastructure.

The plugins are products. Round-Up API is the layer beneath them — the same hardened pipeline any checkout can embed, without building payments, idempotency, and reconciliation from scratch. We made one decision differently from everyone else: the fee comes from the merchant, never the donation.

💯

The charity keeps everything

Your platform fee is billed to you and settled separately. Every cent a customer rounds up arrives at the cause — on every tier, with no exceptions.

🔌

Runs on any checkout

Shopify Plus, WooCommerce, custom checkouts and POS. Platform-agnostic infrastructure rather than a plugin tied to one ecosystem.

📒

Provable, to the cent

Settlement-first execution and a tamper-evident ledger mean money collected always equals money disbursed — and you can audit every movement.

04 Why it's trustworthy

Built like financial infrastructure, because it is.

A giving product lives and dies on trust. Underneath the simple checkout prompt is a pipeline engineered so money is never lost, double-counted, or sent on a payment that later reverses.

⛓️

Settlement-first execution

A round-up only commits once the underlying payment confirms settlement — never on a reversed or abandoned order.

🔁

Idempotent by design

Every event is fingerprinted, so duplicate webhooks or retries can never double-charge a customer or double-send a donation.

📖

Double-entry audit ledger

Every movement is recorded as two equal, opposite postings — append-only and tamper-evident, so the books always balance.

🏦

Verified disbursement

Funds reach charities through a Donor-Advised Fund, so merchants carry no charitable registration or compliance burden.

05 The roadmap

Charity is the wedge. The pipeline is the platform.

The same engine that routes a round-up to a charity can route it anywhere. Charity giving lets us start in a low-regulatory environment while we harden the infrastructure — then open it to the destinations merchants ask for next.

NOW

Charity giving

Round-ups to vetted charities, with 100% reaching the cause. The trust-first entry point.

NEXT

Loyalty points

Route spare change into a merchant's own rewards ledger — deepening retention, not just goodwill.

LATER

Carbon offsets

Convert round-ups into verified offsets from certified registries, with the same reconciliation guarantees.

VISION

Micro-savings

Per-customer savings wallets — the highest-value destination, approached deliberately with the right licensing.
